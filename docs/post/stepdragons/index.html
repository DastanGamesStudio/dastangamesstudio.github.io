Steps & Dragons

June 23, 2019

Trick your friend into falling into devious traps. Use hints to choose the best steps, run to the top, discover hidden dragons and be the first to find the magic scroll in this mystical land of monks! Do you know your friends well enough? Find out in a game of Steps & Dragons!

Features:

  • Real-time online multiplayer for 2 players.
  • Single-player daily challenge mode
  • Upgradable and powerful spell cards
